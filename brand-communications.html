Why It Matters

Brand is your LLM citation strategy

Before AI can cite your brand, it needs to recognise it as an entity — a clearly named, consistently described, authoritative presence on a specific topic. That's brand strategy with a GEO layer built in.

LLMs don't cite vague brands. They cite the ones that show up clearly, repeatedly, and authoritatively across multiple independent sources.

→Consistent naming across every page, profile, and publication builds entity recognition inside LLM knowledge graphs.
→Clear positioning means the model can associate your brand with a specific topic — and surface it when that topic comes up.
→Third-party mentions driven by a genuine communications strategy are the external validation signals LLMs weight most heavily.

FAQ

Common questions

Answers to the questions we get asked before every brand engagement.

What does a brand strategy engagement with Mad Hat Communications include?+
A brand strategy engagement typically includes a brand audit, audience and competitor research, brand architecture, messaging hierarchy, tone of voice guidelines, and a visual identity system. We deliver a full brand playbook your team can execute independently.
How does brand strategy affect LLM citation and AI search visibility?+
LLMs build entity associations — they connect brand names with specific topics, industries, and authority signals. A clearly defined brand with consistent messaging across web, press, and community platforms is significantly more likely to be cited by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude when answering relevant queries.
How long does a brand strategy project take?+
A full brand strategy engagement typically runs 6–10 weeks. This includes discovery and research (weeks 1–2), strategy development (weeks 3–5), creative expression (weeks 6–8), and delivery and handover (weeks 9–10). Scope varies by complexity.
Does Mad Hat Communications work with early-stage startups on brand strategy?+
Yes. We work with brands at all stages — from pre-launch startups building their identity from scratch to established companies repositioning for a new market or audience. Our framework scales to fit the stage and budget of the engagement.
